BCS

British Computing Society Institute

Founded in 1956, BCS has played an important role in educating and nurturing IT professionals, computer scientists, computer engineers, upholding the profession, accrediting chartered IT professional status, and creating a global community active in promoting and furthering the field and practice of computing. BCS, The Chartered Institute for IT, formerly known as the British Computer Society until 2009, is a professional body and a learned society that represents those working in information technology and computer science, both in the United Kingdom and internationally. Entry requirements are : 5 Olevels including Math and English,  We offer Certificate in IT(9months), Diploma in IT(12 months) and Professional Graduate Diploma(12months).

 For more information please Visit www.bcs.org
